ABOUT

the PLAYLIST — The K-Pop Music Poster Series is a self-initiated project by Jihun, inspired by his enduring love for K-Pop music. As a designer, he draws creative energy from K-Pop songs, transforming elements like titles, lyrics, and rhythms into typographic poster designs. This website serves as an archive to enhance the listening experience for fans and celebrate the artistry of K-Pop.
